\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 And I think that\'s just, frankly, a reflection.\xc2\xa0 One of the problems that we have is we\'ve built so many sunsets into the tax code and into the -- so-called the sustainable growth rate formula under Medicare that there\'s this awkwardness between the technical law, which is implausible -- all of the tax cuts disappear, the physician payments are reduced by 20 percent, and what is commonly understood as policy as it currently stands.\xc2\xa0 Our legislation says let\'s take policy as it currently stands, and make sure that you have "pay as you go" off of that.
