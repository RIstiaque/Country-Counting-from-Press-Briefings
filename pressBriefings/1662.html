Q\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 You\'ve said that the goal is to have this done by the end of the year.\xc2\xa0 I understand that as a goal, but can you explain why, given the history and the complexity to this, why that\'s feasible, how that\'s feasible?

GIBBS:\xc2\xa0 Well, again, I think it\'s feasible largely because we can\'t wait for it to happen.\xc2\xa0 The problem has only gotten more intense over the past few years.\xc2\xa0 Getting sick eats into people\'s retirements.\xc2\xa0 It\'s eating into the federal budget.\xc2\xa0 It affects millions of families that are watching the cost of their health care skyrocket.\xc2\xa0 And he believes that all the stakeholders are poised and ready to act, and I think that\'s why today was an important first step in ensuring that that process moves forward.
